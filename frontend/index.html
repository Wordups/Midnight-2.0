Capabilities

Everything a policy ops team needs

Built to eliminate manual effort between a legacy document and a final audit-ready output.

01
Migrate Policy
Upload a legacy .docx, .txt, or .md. AI extracts every field, section, bullet, table, and revision entry — rebuilds it into the target template.
02
Create Policy
Structured intake with smart date defaults and live preview. Fill in what you know — Midnight handles layout and template fidelity.
03
Framework Mapped
Every policy automatically mapped to HIPAA, HiTrust, PCI DSS, ISO 27001, NIST CSF, CoBIT, and SOC 2. Gaps flagged with suggested language.
04
GRC Summary
A second output alongside the policy — full control mapping, gap analysis, and suggested language ready to import into your GRC tool.
05
Live Preview
Review every extracted field, definition, procedure, and framework mapping before generating. No surprises in the final document.
06
Audit Ready
When an auditor asks a question, you have the answer — which policy covers it, which control it maps to, and when it was last reviewed.
